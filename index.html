Accordion Facts

  1. The accordion has a long history.
    • It was invented in the early 1800s.
    • It became popular across Europe.
    • It spread to the Americas through immigrants.
    • It is used in many folk traditions.
  2. There are different types of accordions.
    • Piano accordion
    • Button accordion
    • Chromatic and diatonic types
  3. The accordion produces sound using bellows.
    • Air is pushed through reeds
    • Reeds vibrate to create sound
    • Push/pull controls dynamics
  4. It is used in many music styles.
    • Folk
    • Polka
    • Norteño
    • French musette

How I Started Playing

I remember going to Christmas parties with my dad's side of the family, and my grandpa would always play Christmas songs on the accordion while we sang along. One year when I was around 12, I asked my grandpa if I could borrow one of his accordions. He said yes! I already knew how to play the piano side of the accordion, so my dad taught me the button side on the left. From then on, I have loved playing the accordion!
